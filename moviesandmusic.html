Escape it All

Music, my first love. Movies, my second. My siblings and I used to play my moms old records when were kids and I have the greatest memories of us singing and dancing around the living room together. I grew up listening to everything from country to hip hop. I am still a huge fan of 80’s music! I love me some Michael Jackson and Prince but my go to song is “(Your Love Keeps Lifting Me) Higher and Higher” by Jackie Wilson. Every time I am down, I can play that song and it makes me fill better. Try to listen to it and not feel good after. I have also grown up loving movies and remember watch them on my aunt’s laser disc (bet most of you have no idea what that is). Movies were also a way of escaping the world around me. My top five movies are 1. The Color Purple 2. Dances With Wolves 3. Braveheart 4. Heaven and Earth and 5. The Truman Show. I can watch them over and over again. I really love comedies as well, but the ones listed are my favorites of all time.
