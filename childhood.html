Childhood

We all have things in our lives that we have had to overcome, and my life has been no different. I am the second oldest child born to my mother who was a single parent to me and my four other siblings. My father died in a car accident when I was just five years old and it kind of sent my mother into a tailspin. My life became very unstable and during that time, my mother had my three younger siblings, whom me and my older sister had to help raise. When I was about eight years old, my mother got things back in order and my life changed for the better.
